The Story of NewJeans

The South Korean girl group NewJeans made their debut in 2022 under the banner of ADOR, a sub-company of HYBE. The five members of the group have captured the attention of fans all over the world with their unique take on the K-pop sound.

With their own sound, NewJeans offers a novel interpretation of K-pop by fusing elements of earlier decades with contemporary pop. Being young, creative, and genuine is essential to their approach, which is striking a chord with their expanding fan base.

Members

  • Minji (Leader)
  • Hanni
  • Danielle
  • Haerin
  • Hyein

Their chemistry and dancing ability is loved by everyone all around the world and its only growing as the days go by.

With their energetic concerts and great tracks, NewJeans has already created waves. With its young enthusiasm and superb craftsmanship, their debut single "Attention" became an instant smash.
