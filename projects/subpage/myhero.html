Shigeru Miyamoto: Creator of Iconic Games

Interests Page | Hero Page

Shigeru Miyamoto is a video game designer and producer, widely considered one of the most influential figures in the gaming industry. He is the mind behind some of the most iconic franchises, such as "Super Mario," "The Legend of Zelda," and "Donkey Kong." His work has shaped the industry and brought joy to millions of players worldwide.

He is also my inspiration on why I am fascinated with games of today and why I am very interested in making games in the future. There are a lot of gaming companies out there that make tons of games. But him and his company is the only one that is stuck in my mind and is the one that is still an inspiration to me.
