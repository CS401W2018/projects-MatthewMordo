Newjeans

NewJeans is a South Korean girl group formed by ADOR a sub-company by HYBE. They debuted in 2022 and quickly gained popularity with their unique music and style.

Minji

Leader of NewJeans, Minji is known for her leadership and charm.

Hanni

Hanni is admired for her vocals and multi-talented skills.

Danielle

Danielle brings a refreshing presence and great performance energy.

Haerin

Haerin is known for her calm personality and expressive vocals.

Hyein

The youngest member, Hyein is known for her maturity and charisma.

Newjeans

All of them as a group bring out the best of each other and they have made songs that have resonated with everyone all around the world.
