Recognition in the Music Industry

Since their debut, NewJeans has earned numerous accolades for their innovative music, dynamic performances, and widespread influence in the K-pop industry. Here is a list of some of their most notable achievements:

Major Awards

  • 2023 MAMA Awards: Best New Female Artist, Song of the Year ("OMG")
  • 2023 Melon Music Awards: Best Dance Performance - Female Group, Netizen Popularity Award
  • 2023 Golden Disc Awards: Album of the Year ("New Jeans")

Record-Breaking Achievements

NewJeans has set multiple records, both in South Korea and globally. These include:

  1. Fastest K-pop girl group to reach 1 million album sales.
  2. First rookie group to win the "Song of the Year" award in their debut year.
  3. Top 10 in Billboard's Global 200 with their debut single.
