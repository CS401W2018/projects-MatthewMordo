What kind of photos are good to encode using GIF?

  1. Simple graphics, logos, and images with limited colors (up to 256).
  2. Animated images, as GIF supports simple animations.
  3. Graphics with flat areas of color, like icons or basic illustrations.

What is unique about the PNG format?

  1. Supports lossless compression, meaning no quality is lost when saving.
  2. Can handle transparency, allowing for smoother edges and overlays.
  3. Best for images requiring a transparent background or where quality is critical.

How do you resize your photo to the size it should be displayed?

  1. Use photo editing software (like Photoshop, GIMP, or online tools).
  2. Set the desired dimensions (width and height) based on how the image will be used on the web.
  3. Save the resized image in an appropriate format (JPEG, PNG, etc.).

Why should you resize photos using photo editing software instead of resizing it using CSS?

  1. Quality Control: Resizing in editing software allows for better control over image quality and compression, preventing pixelation or artifacts.
  2. Browser Performance: large images can slow down page load times; optimizing images in advance improves overall performance.
  3. Visual Consistency: ensures that images maintain their aspect ratio and visual integrity, providing a more professional appearance.
