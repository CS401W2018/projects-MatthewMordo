What are the positives and negatives of hosting your videos on YouTube?

Massive Audience Reach: YouTube has over 2 billion logged-in monthly users, giving you access to a global audience.

Free Hosting: YouTube provides free video hosting with virtually unlimited storage for your video

Monetization: You can earn money through ads, sponsorships, or memberships once you reach the required threshold for YouTube’s Partner Program.

Searchability & Discoverability: YouTube videos are indexed by Google, making it easier for your content to be found in search results.

What is the most useful thing you can do with a transform?

In CSS, the transform property is used to modify the position, size, and shape of an element. It allows you to apply 2D and 3D transformations, such as rotating, scaling, translating, and skewing an element, without affecting its layout in the document.

How do you stay safe online?

Use Strong, Unique Passwords: Avoid using easily guessable passwords. Utilize a password manager to generate and store secure passwords for different accounts.

Enable Two-Factor Authentication (2FA): This adds an extra layer of security by requiring a second form of verification (e.g., SMS code or app-generated code).

Beware of Phishing Scams: Don’t click on suspicious links in emails or text messages. Always verify the sender’s identity before responding.

Keep Software Updated: Install updates for your operating system, apps, and security software regularly to protect against vulnerabilities.
