What are different ways to style your form?

  • You can use padding, margin, alignment, form width, font and text styling, Input field styling, and many more.

Why does the professor like to use flex?

  • Because it simplifies the layouts and allows you to align form elements (inputs, labels, buttons) in rows or columns without needing complex CSS tricks (like floats or inline-blocks).
