The header usually contains the website's logo, site title, and navigation links (nav).

What goes in Main?

The main section contains the primary content of the webpage. This includes text, images, videos, and any other content relevant to the specific page, such as articles, product descriptions, or features.

What goes in the Footer?

The footer usually contains copyright information, additional navigation links (like privacy policy, terms of service), contact details, and sometimes social media links or a site map.

It can also include newsletter sign-up forms or other relevant information.
